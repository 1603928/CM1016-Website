    Application Deadlines

    The admission points for research degree applicants are October and February of each academic session. Please note that the application deadline for February 2017 entry has now passed. If you select the February option on either application or submission, the system will default your application to 1 February 2018 entry. The next entry points are:

    If you wish to be considered for October 2017 entry, you must submit a complete application by 31 May 2017.

    If you wish to be considered for February 2018 entry, you must submit a complete application by 31 October 2017.

    Please ensure that you meet our minimum English language requirements prior to applying. Information on our English language requirements

    Doctorate Routes Available

    Masters degree (MSc/MRes):

    Full time research

    Normally 12 months full-time or 24 months part-time

    Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)

    Full time research

    Normally 36 months full-time or 60 months part-time

    Professional Doctorate (PD)

    Combination of taught modules and research project

    Normally 36 months full-time or 60 months part-time
