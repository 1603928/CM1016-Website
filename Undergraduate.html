    • Studying for an undergraduate degree can be a challenging, but rewarding experience and can open up a variety of career options to you.

    You can enter university straight from school, college or as a mature student. An honours undergraduate degree will take four years to complete full-time.

    Applications for undergraduate courses must be made through UCAS. You can apply online at www.ucas.com/apply. When you apply you will require our institution code name and number:

    • Institution code name: RGU
    • Institution code number: R36

    Once you register and create your username and password you are guided through the process of applying. After submission, log in to your UCAS Track account and you can see the progress of your application, view and respond to your offers.
